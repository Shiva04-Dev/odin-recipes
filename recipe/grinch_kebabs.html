Grinch Kebabs

Description

These little fruit skewers are simple to make and a great addition to your appetizers at any Christmas party.


Ingerdients

  • 24 Green Grapes
  • 1 Large Banana, Chopped into 24 Slices
  • 24 Hulled Strawberries
  • 24 Miniture Marshmellows
  • 24 Toothpicks

Steps

  1. Thread 1 grape, 1 banana slice, 1 strawberry (narrow end facing up), and 1 marshmallow onto 1 toothpick to resemble the Grinch's head in his red hat.
  2. Repeat 24 times
  3. Serve and Enjoy
